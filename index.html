Statement

Writing as a test of conditions.

ΔIf is a collection of essays, fragments, and meditations that explore what remains when a single condition is removed, reversed, or intensified. Rather than seeking conclusions, each piece follows the consequences of an assumption until it begins to fray.

These writings are meant to be read slowly, revisited, and kept open. They are not answers. They are deliberate experiments in attention.
